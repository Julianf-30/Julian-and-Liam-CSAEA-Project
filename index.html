How does Chess work?

Chess piece How it moves
Pawn Forward 1 space, captures diagonally
Queen All directions 1 space at a time
Knight 2 spaces one way, perpindicular another way (L shape)
King All directions 1 space at a time
Bishop Diagonally, any amount of spaces
Rook + Shape, any amount of squares
